OCIS: BISCUIT - Causal Representation Learning from Binary Interactions

Phillip Lippe
Project Slides Video Paper Demo
BISCUIT can be applied to realistic Embodied AI environment to identify the causal variables.

Abstract

Together with Sara Magliacane, I was invited to give a talk on our recent work BISCUIT. Sara gave an introduction on Causal Representation Learning for Temporal Sequences. In the second part, I gave a detailed overview of BISCUIT, including the the theory, the architecture, and our experimental results. For more details, check out our paper and project page.
